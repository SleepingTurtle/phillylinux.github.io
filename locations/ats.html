Transportation:

The Malvern and Paoli stations on SEPTA's Paoli/Thorndale Line (see the PDF schedule) are closest to the site, but they are not within convenient walking distance. If you would like to attend but can only use public transportation, please send a note to the PLUG discussion list and someone will probably be able to pick you up.
