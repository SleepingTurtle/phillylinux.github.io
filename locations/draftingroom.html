Directions to The Drafting Room - Exton

Where:

The Drafting room - Exton
Colonial 100 Shoppes and Offices
635 N. Pottsdown Pike
Exton, PA 19341
We should have a room reserved under "PLUG".

The Drafting Room has a GREAT assortment of beer, with around a dozen microbrews on tap and over a hundred bottled varieties. They also have a beer engine to dispense cask-conditioned real ale.

Driving
Directions:

From the PA Turnpike:

Take exit 312 for Downingtown (old exit number 23). Take PA Route 100 South around 2.5 miles. Look the for Colonial Village 100 shopping center on the left (east) side of the road.

From US 202 and the south

Take US 202 to US 30 West. Take US 30 West to PA Route 100 North. Follow 100 north around 2 miles. Look for the Colonial Village 100 shopping center on the right (east) side of the road, just past Ship Road.

Turn-by-Turn Directions

If you would like more detailed driving directions or wish to view street maps of the location, try Google Maps.

Public
Transportation:

The Septa R5 Exton station (see the weekday schedule) is the closest to The Drafting Room. If you intend to arrive by public transportation, you will probably need a ride. Please send a message to rsvp@phillylinux.org and we'll help coordinate a lift from and to the station.

Contact Information:

 The Drafting Room can be contacted by phone at (610) 363-0521. Their website is located at http://draftingroom.com/exton/ex_home.html
