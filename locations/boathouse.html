Directions to Flanigan's Boathouse from the Malvern Unisys Office

Where:

Flanigan's Boathouse
16 Great Valley Parkway
Malvern, Pennsylvania 19355

After the PLUG West meeting finishes, some of us head to the nearby Flanigan's Boathouse for beer (or a non-beer beverage of choice) and more Linux talk. Flanigan's has a nice assortment of microbrews on tap and is usually pretty quiet by the time the PLUG West meeting has finished.

Driving
Directions:

From Unisys Malvern (2476 Swedesford Rd., Malvern, PA)

Turn left out of the Unisys parking lot onto Swedesford Road. Follow Swedesford to PA-29. Turn right onto PA-29 North, a.k.a. Morehall Road. Go north on 29 to Great Valley Parkway. Turn left onto Great Valley Parkway. Turn into the first possible driveway on your left to enter the parking lot for Flanigan's Boathouse.
