Notation (review)

Keys

  • Control keys: C-u
  • Meta keys: M-w (use Alt or Esc key)
  • Commands: M-x command-name
  • Other keys: RET

Configuration

  • .emacs (in home directory)

Key binding

  • During an editing session: M-x global-set-key
  • From within .emacs file: (global-set-key [(meta control l)] 'some-command)
