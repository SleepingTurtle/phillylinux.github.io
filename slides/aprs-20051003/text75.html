Very Bad Paths

  • WIDE, RELAY

    • Digipeated by high WIDE digipeater
    • Then Digipeated by all RELAY digipeaters in range of WIDE = flood of duplicate packets

  • WIDE,RELAY,GATE

    • Digipeated by high WIDE digipeater
    • Then digipeated by all RELAY digipeaters in range
    • Then all these packets are transmitted over a 300 baud HF gate. Thus the HF gate tied up for 10 minutes from one packet....
